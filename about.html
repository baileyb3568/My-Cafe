About Velvet Roast

A Modern Café Built Around Warmth & Comfort

Founded in 2026, Velvet Roast is a modern café serving handcrafted coffee, fresh food, and signature drinks in a warm and welcoming space. From smooth espresso and specialty lattes to freshly baked pastries, cakes, and handcrafted paninis, every menu item is carefully prepared by our chefs and baristas with quality and everyday comfort in mind. The café is designed with soft interiors and relaxed seating. Velvet Roast was created as a place that people genuinely enjoy spending time in whether you are stopping by for a quiet morning coffee, a midday lunch, or an afternoon catch up with friends. Inspired by modern café culture and slow everyday moments, the space blends comforting surroundings with carefully crafted food and drinks to create an experience that feels both stylish and welcoming.

Our Story

More Than Just Coffee

Founded in 2026, Velvet Roast was created with the idea of bringing together quality coffee, fresh food, and a calm everyday atmosphere in one welcoming space. Inspired by modern cafés, slow coffee culture, and warm social environments, the café was designed to feel comfortable from the moment people walk through the door.

What started as a vision for a stylish yet relaxed café quickly became a space centred around handcrafted drinks, freshly prepared food, and genuine everyday moments. From morning espresso runs and midday brunches to late afternoon conversations with friends, Velvet Roast was built to be more than just a quick coffee stop it was created as a place people genuinely enjoy spending time in. Every detail, from the soft interiors and relaxed seating to the carefully prepared menu, reflects the café’s focus on comfort, quality, and creating an atmosphere that feels welcoming to everyone.
